uniONE

Connect with like-minded peers and pursue your passions

Description

uniONE is a platform designed to help university students connect and build relationships based on shared interests and activities. Students can create a profile that showcases their hobbies and skills, and availability, which the app uses to match them with other students. With uniONE, students can find like-minded individuals and take part in activities they both enjoy. The app streamlines the process of getting to know each other, allowing students to send questions and answers before meeting in person. To ensure a successful meeting, both matched students will receive unique identification cards. uniONE is specifically targeted towards university students at the University of Alberta, and is available on both iPhone and Android through the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.
